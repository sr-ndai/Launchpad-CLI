## Observations

- The most conservative interpretation of Phase 2 discovery was to scan only
  the top-level `INPUT_DIR` for matching extensions. Recursive discovery and
  Nastran INCLUDE parsing remain out of scope.
- The Nastran run command is a deterministic local contract for later submit
  work, not a live cluster validation. Real remote execution behavior will be
  exercised by later submission tasks.
- ANSYS remains intentionally unimplemented beyond the shared contract so the
  abstraction is stable without guessing an unsupported workflow.
